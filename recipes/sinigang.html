Sinigang

One of my all time favorite Filipino dishes that I cook whenever I'm home sick. Can be made with pork, beef, or catfish.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 pound beef stew meat, cut into 1 inch cubes
  • 1 quart water
  • 2 large tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 pound fresh green beans, rinced and trimmed
  • 1/2 medium head bok choy, cut into 1 1/2 inch strips
  • 1 head fresh broccoli, cut into bit size pieces
  • 1 package tamarind soup base

Directions:

  1. Heat oil in a medium stock pot and then saute onion and garlic until tender.
  2. Add your protein of choice into the pot and saute until browned.
  3. Pour in water, bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 20 to 30 minutes.
  4. Place tomatoes and green beans in pot and continue to simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in bok choy, broccoli and tamarind soup mix and simmer for 10 more minutes.
  6. Serve with white rice and enjoy!
